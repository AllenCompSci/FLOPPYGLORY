Pacman X-Treme is an action packed modification version of its original.  
Playersare challenged with 6 levels of consuming yellow pellets and avoiding ghosts.
In between levels, players get money to purchase items in which change game play 
and increase the chance that the player is succesful.
Pacman X-treme can save and load play files of previous games, so you can start 
where you left off the last time you played.
Turn on your computer and have fun with this awesome graphically designed game!
