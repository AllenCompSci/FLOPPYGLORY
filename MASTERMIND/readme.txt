Mastermind
Instructions

1. Using the numbers indicated on the pegs, fill in the blank 
circles by pressing those numbers on the main keyboard.
2. To quit the game, hold down one of the playable numbers until 
all the blank circles are filled.
3. Use the numbers beside any function to select that option.
In the small circles during game play:
4. Black circles mean the peg is the correct color in the
correct slot.
5. White circles mean the peg is the correct color in the 
wrong slot.
6. A blank circle means the color isn't used.
